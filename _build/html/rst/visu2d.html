class Visu.label_2D.PEAKPICK(data, display, convert, paramz)¶

Peakpicking

baryc_far()¶

Makes the points far from the self.barycenter. self.lx, self.ly defined from a small division fo the window dimensions.

barycenter_method()¶

Takes the labels far one from another and all the labels far from the peaks 1) Begins by making a self.barycenter and getting the labels at the opposite of the vector ‘peak to self.barycenter’ at the distance dist_min. 2) Makes a correction on the labels ovelapping the peaks 3) Makes a correction on the labels ovelapping each other.
